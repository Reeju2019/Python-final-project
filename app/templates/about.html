About Us

This Heart Disease Risk Prediction tool is developed by a team of dedicated researchers and data scientists aiming to provide accessible and reliable health diagnostics through technology. Our application leverages advanced machine learning algorithms to predict the risk of heart disease based on a variety of health indicators and personal information provided by users.

The project is a part of our broader initiative to enhance healthcare outcomes through innovative technology solutions. By offering this tool, we hope to assist individuals in gaining a better understanding of their heart health and encourage proactive health management.
